A feedback loop for learning

If you pick up your phone and call a friend and talk for an hour, your telco will know more about you in that one hour than your schools did in the 20+ years that they hosted you.

In the absence of that knowledge about individuals, universities are bound to provide one-size-fits-all curriculums that don't scale.

It's time to build a feedback loop for learning, as a foundation for optimising education and achieving one education system per person, so every student can work effectively with others to be the best she/he can possibly be, without competing or get compared with anyone but her/himself.
